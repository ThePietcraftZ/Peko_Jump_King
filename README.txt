HOW TO INSTALL: 

-Go to your JumpKing directory (either manually or through steam)

-Open the "Content" folder (C:...\Jump King\Content)

-Pull in the "audio" and "king" folder from the mod into your Jumpking directory -> "content" (C:...\Jump King\Content)

-It will ask you to replace 5 files click "yes"

-Done

HOW TO DEINSTALL:

-Delete the "audio" and the "king" folder in your jump king directory

-Let steam check for missing files

-Download missing files

-Done
